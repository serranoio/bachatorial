### A continuation of isolation exercises

The reason why we do the pure isolation exercise is so that we can combine them together with proper technique to do these beautiful moves.

#### cambre

A cambre is a chest isolation that combines the y & z plane. Add in changes in body weight. To make it more pronounced.

_INSERT VIDEO_

:::tip
 Prepping the other way is a principle of dance that is common amongst a lot of moves. For example, when turning to the right, you prep left. When wanting to go up, you prep by going down first.
:::
